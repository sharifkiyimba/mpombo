The story of Mpombo Family Restaurant is a story of love — for Uganda, community, and the ancient flavours of our homeland.

✦ Founded in Lyantonde

Born from Ugandan Roots

Mpombo Family Restaurant started as a dream — to bring the authentic flavours of Ugandan home cooking to a proper table. Located opposite Petro Uganda in Lyantonde District, we opened our doors with one mission: cook real food, the real way.

We source our matoke from local farmers, our tilapia fresh from Lake Victoria, and our groundnuts from surrounding villages. Every dish is cooked slowly, with care, using methods passed through generations of Ugandan kitchens.

Today, we're proud to be part of the Lyantonde community — feeding families, hosting celebrations, and keeping the tradition of Ugandan hospitality alive.
